6-Day Ol Pejeta, Lake Nakuru & Masai Mara Safari

6 Days / 5 Nights  |  Ol Pejeta, Lake Nakuru & Masai Mara

About This Package

Six unforgettable days await across three of Kenya’s most spectacular wildlife destinations. Journey from the rhino sanctuaries of Ol Pejeta Conservancy to the flamingo-fringed shores of Lake Nakuru, then on to the endless golden plains of the world-famous Masai Mara. Every day combines close wildlife encounters, comfortable stays, and effortless, personal travel.

What to Expect

Six days of all-inclusive adventure await, rich with wildlife, striking scenery, and moments of genuine connection with nature.

  • Immersive Wildlife Encounters — thrilling game drives across Kenya’s top reserves, with chances to spot the Big Five, rare black and white rhinos, chimpanzees, flamingos, and more
  • A Seamless, All-Inclusive Experience — guided safaris, park fees, 4×4 transport, and round-the-clock support designed for effortless travel
  • Stunning Landscapes & Scenic Drives — journey through the Masai Mara savannahs, Ol Pejeta’s lush plains, and the breathtaking Great Rift Valley

Accommodation

Carefully selected mid-range properties, well located for wildlife viewing and comfortable full-board stays.

  • Days 1–2: Kongoni Nanyuki
  • Day 3: Lake Nakuru Sopa Lodge
  • Days 4–5: Mara Sopa Lodge
  • Day 6: Depart

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 — Arrival & Transfer to Ol Pejeta Conservancy

    Karibu Kenya! On arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, your guide will greet you, assist with your luggage, and introduce the days ahead. Settle in for a scenic 2.5–3 hour drive through villages and farmland to Ol Pejeta Conservancy. Stop at the Equator crossing in Nanyuki for a local demonstration of the Coriolis effect before continuing to the conservancy.

    A welcome drink and fresh lunch await, followed by an introduction to Ol Pejeta’s conservation work. As the afternoon cools, head out on your first game drive, with strong chances of seeing black rhino, elephant, and antelope. End with dinner under the stars.

    Meals: Full Board

  2. Day 2 — Full Day Exploring Ol Pejeta

    Start with a hearty breakfast before a full day exploring Ol Pejeta’s remarkable landscape, where conservation and wildlife viewing come together. Visit the Sweetwaters Chimpanzee Sanctuary, home to rescued chimpanzees, and enjoy a relaxed picnic lunch surrounded by nature. Continue with afternoon game viewing and photography before returning to camp for dinner and stories around the fire.

    Meals: Full Board

  3. Day 3 — Morning Game Drive & Transfer to Lake Nakuru

    Depart early for the 3–4 hour drive to Lake Nakuru National Park, enjoying dramatic Great Rift Valley views along the way. After arrival and lunch, head into an afternoon game drive through a park famed for prolific birdlife, including flamingos that often line the lakeshore. Settle in for dinner and a restful night.

    Meals: Full Board

  4. Day 4 — Lake Nakuru Game Drive & Transfer to the Masai Mara

    After breakfast and check-out, enjoy one more Lake Nakuru game drive, with opportunities to see the park’s white rhinos and abundant birdlife. Continue on the 5–6 hour journey to the Masai Mara. On arrival, check in, unwind, and have a late lunch before an optional afternoon game drive for a first glimpse of the famous Big Five. End with dinner under the wide African sky.

    Meals: Full Board

  5. Day 5 — Full Day in the Masai Mara

    Wake to the sounds of the wild and enjoy breakfast before a full day exploring the Masai Mara’s vast savannahs with a picnic lunch. Look for lion, cheetah, elephant, and wildebeest and, depending on the season, the spectacle of the Great Migration. Your guide will share insight into the ecosystem and regional conservation before you return in the late afternoon for a final dinner.

    Meals: Full Board

  6. Day 6 — Departure to Nairobi

    After breakfast, begin your journey back to Nairobi, carrying memories of an unforgettable Kenyan safari.
