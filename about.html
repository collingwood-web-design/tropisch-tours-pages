About Tropisch Tours & Travel

At Tropisch Tours & Travel, we believe travel should be authentic, inspiring and stress free. Rooted in the warm hospitality and rich heritage of East Africa, our signature expertise begins at home and extends across the globe. We design masterfully curated, end-to-end travel experiences that connect discerning travelers with Africa’s iconic landscapes and the world’s most captivating international destinations.

We design and manage every detail of your itinerary from start to finish, providing a single, trusted point of contact for your entire journey. By taking full accountability for your logistics, comfort, and safety, we eliminate the stress of planning so you can focus entirely on the wonder of exploration.

Founded on a passion for authentic exploration, we blend immersive local discovery with uncompromising comfort. Whether you are looking to witness the raw majesty of an African safari or embark on a bespoke expedition across continents, we orchestrate flawless journeys tailored precisely to your vision.

From our Nairobi headquarters, our elite, multilingual team delivers world-class global support—proudly serving local travelers and welcoming the world to Africa.
