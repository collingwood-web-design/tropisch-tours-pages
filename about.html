About Us

Your Nairobi-based partner for safaris, coast, and custom travel across Africa.

From the Maasai Mara to the Indian Ocean, we design personal journeys rooted in local knowledge — for travellers who want authenticity, comfort, and care at every step.

Our Story

Founded on a passion for Africa’s incredible landscapes, wildlife, and culture, we specialise in crafting personalised safari and travel packages that combine authenticity with comfort. Whether you are chasing the wildebeest migration across the Maasai Mara, watching elephants roam beneath Kilimanjaro in Amboseli, or unwinding on the white sands of Mombasa or Zanzibar, we are with you every step of the way.

From our Nairobi office, experienced guides in English, Spanish, French, and German support travellers from Kenya and around the world.

Sharing authentic Maasai experiences with our local partners.

What We Do

We design and operate a wide range of safari and travel experiences tailored to both local and international travellers.

Wildlife Safaris

Across Kenya’s iconic national parks and game reserves.

Beach Holidays

Travel Logistics

Every traveller is different. Share your ideas with us and we will design a safari, coast escape, or multi-destination itinerary around your dates, interests, and budget — handled with the same care from your first message to your journey home.
