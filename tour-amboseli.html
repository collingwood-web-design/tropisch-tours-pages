Amboseli Wilderness Safari

3 Days / 2 Nights  |  Ol Tukai Lodge, Amboseli

About This Package

At the foot of Africa’s greatest mountain lies one of Kenya’s most breathtaking wildlife sanctuaries. Amboseli National Park, home to some of the largest free-roaming elephant herds on the continent, offers a safari experience unlike any other — where iconic wildlife roams against the majestic backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro. This exclusive 3-day package places you right in the heart of it all, with luxury accommodation at Ol Tukai Lodge, daily private game drives, and seamless door-to-door transport in a fully equipped 4×4 Land Cruiser.

Wildlife Highlights

  • Amboseli’s legendary large elephant herds — some of the biggest in Africa
  • Iconic views of Mount Kilimanjaro as your safari backdrop
  • Lions, cheetahs, zebras, wildebeest, giraffes and over 400 bird species
  • Vast open plains offering exceptional year-round game viewing

What’s Included

  • Amboseli National Park entry fees
  • Private luxury 4×4 Land Cruiser with professional English-speaking driver-guide (other languages available)
  • Door-to-door pickup and drop-off from your place of residence in Nairobi
  • 2 nights full board accommodation at Ol Tukai Lodge, Amboseli
  • All meals — breakfast, lunch and dinner throughout
  • 2 private game drives per day — morning and evening
  • Bottled water throughout the journey and game drives
  • All applicable taxes

Not Included

  • Optional activities and personal excursions
  • Personal expenses — drinks, laundry, calls
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Travel insurance

Ideal For

  • Couples & Honeymooners
  • Families
  • Solo Travellers
  • Groups
  • Wildlife Enthusiasts
  • Photography Lovers
