Maasai Mara Wilderness Safari

3 Days / 2 Nights  |  Mara Intrepids Lodge, Masai Mara

About This Package

Experience the breathtaking beauty of the world-famous Masai Mara, home to Africa’s most iconic wildlife and stunning landscapes. From thrilling game drives to unforgettable sunsets over the savannah, this safari offers the perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and authentic African experiences.

Package Highlights

  • Exciting game drives in the Masai Mara National Reserve
  • Opportunity to spot the Big Five
  • Spectacular wildlife viewing and photography
  • Scenic landscapes and breathtaking sunsets
  • Optional visit to a traditional Maasai village
  • Professional driver-guide throughout the safari

What’s Included

  • Masai Mara National Park entry fees
  • Private luxury 4×4 Land Cruiser with professional English-speaking driver-guide (other languages available)
  • Door-to-door pickup and drop-off from your place of residence in Nairobi
  • 2 nights full board accommodation
  • All meals — breakfast, lunch and dinner throughout
  • 2 private game drives per day — morning and evening
  • Bottled water throughout the journey and game drives

Not Included

  • Optional activities and personal excursions
  • Personal expenses — drinks, laundry, calls
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Travel insurance
  • Any items not specifically mentioned under inclusions

Ideal For

  • Couples & Honeymooners
  • Families
  • Solo Travellers
  • Groups
  • Wildlife Enthusiasts
  • Photography Lovers
