5-Day Diani Beach Getaway by SGR

5 Days / 4 Nights  |  Diani Beach, South Coast

About This Package

Trade the city for the coast on this five-day, four-night escape to Diani Beach, travelling in comfort aboard Kenya’s Standard Gauge Railway. Glide past changing landscapes on the way to Mombasa, then unwind on the powder-white sands of the South Coast, where turquoise waters and warm ocean breeze set the pace for a few days of pure relaxation. It’s the easiest way to trade routine for reef views — no driving, no stress, just an effortless coastal escape.

What to Expect

  • Comfortable, scenic rail journey — a smooth 4.5-hour SGR ride with panoramic views and on-board refreshments
  • Beachfront relaxation — stay at a resort with direct beach access, inviting pools, and comfortable rooms
  • A genuine coastal escape — from sunrise swims to sunset dhow cruises, every day moves at your own pace

Optional adventures nearby include snorkeling, jet skiing, dolphin tours, and excursions to Wasini Island (at extra cost).

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 — Nairobi to Diani

    Your coastal escape begins with a morning transfer to the Nairobi SGR Terminus for a smooth, scenic 4.5-hour train ride to Mombasa. On arrival, your driver will be waiting to whisk you off to your beachfront hotel in Diani. Once checked in, ease into island time — settle by the pool, breathe in the ocean air, and let the resort’s amenities set the tone. Round off the day with dinner and a restful first night by the sea.

  2. Day 2 — Full Day of Beach Bliss

    Wake up to the sound of the waves and a breakfast with a view. Today is yours to enjoy exactly as you please — laze by the pool, wade into the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, or take a slow walk along the soft white sand. Feeling active? Jet skiing, paddleboarding, and snorkeling are all on offer nearby (at an extra cost). Wind down with a relaxed dinner back at the resort as the sun sets over the coast.

  3. Day 3 — Adventures Beyond Diani

    After breakfast, use the day to explore a little further afield. Head out to Wasini Island and Kisite Marine Park for snorkeling and a chance to spot dolphins. Enjoy a quiet pace in the late afternoon by visiting Diani’s nearby local art and craft scene. Return to your resort for dinner and a peaceful evening.

  4. Day 4 — Spa, Sunsets & Final Touches

    Ease into a slow morning with breakfast, then spend the day however you like — book a spa treatment, stretch out on the beach, or pick up a few souvenirs to take home. As evening approaches, cap off your stay with a sunset dhow cruise, complete with drinks and snacks as you sail along the coastline. Enjoy a final, memorable dinner in Diani before your journey home.

  5. Day 5 — Diani to Nairobi

    Savour a last breakfast with ocean views before checking out. You’ll be transferred to Mombasa Terminus for your afternoon SGR train back to Nairobi, arriving in the evening refreshed, recharged, and already planning your next coastal escape.
